One tap fires Venmo requests to everyone automatically. Done.

Rally Tally was born out of a real problem — the end-of-night Venmo chaos that every college student knows too well. We built the app we wished existed.

The Mission

Make going out
about the night,
not the math.

Rally Tally is a going-out expense tracking and Venmo settlement app built specifically for the college going-out experience. Not for splitting rent. Not for tracking subscriptions. For actual nights out — the bars, the Ubers, the late-night food runs, the cover charges.

We're targeting college students and Greek life communities across the country — people who go out 2–4 times a week with the same friend groups and have been dealing with the same post-night financial chaos for years.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Information We Collect

Rally Tally collects information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, phone number, and payment information when you create an account or use our services. We also collect information about how you use the app, including the expenses you log, groups you create, and transactions you initiate.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including processing Venmo settlement requests, calculating expense splits, and enabling group expense tracking. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

  • Process your expense tracking and Venmo settlement requests
  • Send you notifications about your active Tallies and settlements
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app experience
  • Send you updates about new features (you can opt out anytime)

Information Sharing

We share your information with other users in your Tally groups as necessary to provide the service — for example, showing group members what expenses have been logged. We do not share your personal information with advertisers or third-party data brokers.

Venmo Integration

Rally Tally integrates with Venmo to facilitate payments. When you use our Venmo settlement feature, we initiate payment requests on your behalf through Venmo's API. Your Venmo credentials are never stored by Rally Tally. Venmo's privacy policy governs how Venmo handles your data.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and protect your account credentials.
